DNA PRECIPITATION PROTOCOL

Reagents Required: 
Ammonium acetate: sala stock reactivos comunes CIPF, Nº 28 y 29 (pedir llave a garita seguridad, planta -1); MW = 77.08 g/mol. Preparar stock 50Ml a 10M.
Sodium acetate: sala stock reactivos comunes CIPF, Nº 375, 376, 377 (pedir llave a garita seguridad, planta -1); MW = 136g/mol. Preparar stock 50 Ml a 2M.
Ice-cold 100% etanol (stock -20ºC).
70% etanol

Process: 
Add 1/5 volume of 10M ammonium acetate (pH 5.0-5.2) or sodium acetate. 
Add 2 volumes of ice-cold 100% ethanol added to DNA solution and mix well.
Leave on ice 15 mins
Centrifugate for 15 min at 13 000 rpm (RT)
Carefully decant the suprenatant without disturbing the pellet.
Wash DNA pellet by adding 1-10Ml (depending on the size of the preparation) of room temperature 70% etanol (v/v) at RT. This removes co-precipitated salt and replaces the isopropanol with the more volatile etanol, making the DNA easier to redissolve.
Centrifugate at 13 000 rpm for 5 min (RT)
Dry in a vacuum centrifuge for 5 to 10 min.
Resuspend in water
